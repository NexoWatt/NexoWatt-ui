Definiert die linke Navigation im SmartHome (Tablet/PC Sidebar, Mobile Drawer).
Felder: id, title, optional icon, viewMode (rooms/functions), Filter: roomIds, funcIds, types, optional favoritesOnly, optional href (Link).
